About Us

Meet the Team

Principal Attorney

TerryAnn attained her Bachelor of Laws degree in 2019 from the University of the West Indies with honours. She then attended the Hugh Wooding Law School where she spent 2 years and was awarded a Legal Education certificate in 2021.

During her time at the Hugh Wooding Law School TerryAnn was the President of the Barbados Student Committee where she assisted many Barbadian students who were stranded at the beginning of the pandemic.

TerryAnn was admitted to practise law in Barbados and became a member of the Barbados Bar Association in 2021. Since then her practice areas have included Litigation, Personal Injuries, Estates, Land matters and Family matters.

What we offer

Our Services

Estate

Creating a will provides you with certainty as to what will happen to your family and assets once you have passed. This is a key way to help your loved ones during a time when they will face emotional distress and rapid change. We know how confusing and emotional the entire process can be. We will therefore work alongside you to understand what is most important to you and your family. Once we have grasped such we will then advise you and assist you in making the right decisions, devising the most appropriate strategy to suit your needs without making it too complex.   

Our services include but are not limited to:
* Estate Planning
* Wills & Codicils
* Will and Probate Searches
* Advising on Legal Entitlements
* Investigating the Assets and Debts of the Deceased
* Grants of Probate and Letters of Administration
* Resealing of Foreign Grants
* Administration of the Estate
* Supervising the Distribution of Assets
* Preparing Documents to Transfer Ownership of the Property of the Deceased

Personal Injuries

An injury can occur when you least expect it, and change your life forever. We understand that the healing process can be long and hard and the last thing you want to deal with is the financial burden caused by the injury and communicating with the party responsible. With the necessary experience and compassion to handle your claim, you can trust that we will focus on getting compensation for you while you focus on healing.

Our scope of personal injury cases include but is not limited to:
* Auto Accidents
* Slip and Fall Accidents
* Workplace Accidents
* Medical Malpractice
* Product Liability/ Product Defects
* Pet Attacks

Our services include but are not limited to:
* Communicating with the party responsible
* Obtaining medical evidence of the extent of the injuries
* Researching the extent of the compensation entitled
* Preparing a Quantified Claim
* Negotiating a monetary settlement

Family Law

Going through a family law matter can be one of the most difficult things you will ever go through in your lifetime. With our professionalism, integrity and compassion, we are able to provide you with confidence in a time when nothing in life seems certain and help you create a custom solution that addresses your needs and priorities, and keeps your family’s well being at the forefront.

Our services include but are not limited to:
* Divorce
* Child Custody
* Child Support & Visitation
* Divorce
* Domestic Violence
* Guardianship
* Separation
* Restraining Orders
* Adoption

Employment Law

Finding yourself in a position where you feel as though you’ve been wrongfully terminated or unfairly treated by an employer is never easy. We aim to focus on the duties owed to you, whether there is a breach of that duty and if there are any available remedies.

Our services include but are not limited to:
*Communicating with your employer
* Researching the extent of the compensation entitled

Civil Law

Finding yourself in the middle of a dispute is never easy. You want a swift and favorable resolution so that you can move on. We focus first on the duties owed among the involved parties as well as analysis of the value of claims in order to obtain the best possible resolution of your case without undue expense or delay.

Our services include but are not limited to:

* Defamation
* Economic Losses
* Breach of Contract
* Employment Litigation
* Negligence
