About Us

Meet the Team

Principal Attorney

Miss TerryAnn Odle attained her Bachelor of Laws Degree with Honours, in 2019, from the University of the West Indies Cave Hill Campus. She then attended the Hugh Wooding Law School and was awarded a Legal Education certificate in 2021. During her time at the Hugh Wooding Law School TerryAnn was the President of the Barbados Student Committee where she assisted many Barbadian students who were stranded at the beginning of the pandemic.

TerryAnn was admitted to practice law in Barbados and became a member of the Barbados Bar Association in 2021. Prior to her pursuit as an independent practitioner, Miss Odle amassed a wealth of knowledge and experience sitting on boards and tribunals. In addition, she worked at a local Law Firm practicing areas such as Litigation, Personal Injuries, Estates, Land Matters and Family Matters.
